Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points?

You earn points primarily by referring friends to GiftRush. When someone signs up using your referral link, you receive 500 points. You can also earn bonus points through special promotions and daily logins.

How many points do I need for a reward?

The points required vary depending on the reward. Gift cards typically range from 2000 points ($10 value) to 10000 points ($50 value). You can view all available rewards and their point costs in your dashboard after signing up.

How long does it take to receive my gift card?

Most gift card codes are delivered instantly to your dashboard and via email after redemption. In some cases, it may take up to 24 hours for our team to process your request, particularly for high-value rewards.

Is GiftRush completely free to use?

Yes, GiftRush is 100% free to use. We never charge any fees for sign-up, referring friends, or redeeming rewards. Our platform is supported by advertisers and partnerships with the brands we offer gift cards for.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No, our system allows only one account per person. We have measures in place to detect multiple accounts from the same IP address or device. Creating multiple accounts is against our terms of service and may result in account suspension.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through the contact form on our website, or by emailing support@giftrush.com. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days.
